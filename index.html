Postpartum Care at Home

At Bright Bloom Wellness, we help mothers heal confidently, safely, and holistically after childbirth. You don't have to struggle alone — our postpartum-trained Nurse Practitioner provides in-home and virtual care to support your physical recovery, emotional health, and newborn transition.

In-Home Postpartum Care

Recovery after childbirth requires careful attention and professional support. New mothers need comprehensive care during the postpartum period, especially in the early weeks when physical recovery and emotional adjustment are critical.

As a Nurse Practitioner specializing in postpartum care, I provide expert in-home visits to assess your recovery, monitor your physical health, and provide guidance on caring for yourself and your newborn.

Telehealth Postpartum Care

For many new mothers, accessing care from the comfort of home is essential. Telehealth consultations provide convenient access to professional postpartum care without leaving your home.

Through secure video consultations, I can assess your recovery progress, answer questions about your physical recovery, provide emotional support, and offer guidance on newborn care. This flexible approach ensures you receive the care you need while maintaining your comfort and privacy.

Meet Your Provider

A Family Nurse Practitioner passionate about postpartum care, women's health, and holistic wellness.

With years of clinical experience in primary care and women's health, I created Bright Bloom Wellness to fill the gap mothers often face after delivery — when they are discharged early, overwhelmed at home, and expected to recover without support.

I provide compassionate, culturally attuned postpartum care with a focus on:

  • Preventing complications
  • Supporting physical & emotional healing
  • Educating and empowering mothers
  • Reducing unnecessary ER visits
  • Helping women transition confidently into motherhood
