What We Offer

Postpartum In-Home Care

DFW Only — Virtual in TX, NM, UT, AZ, NV

Our comprehensive postpartum services include:

  • In-home postpartum checkups (BP, bleeding, incision checks, edema, vitals)
  • C-section incision monitoring
  • Breastfeeding support + referrals if needed
  • Newborn care education
  • Postpartum mental health screening
  • Nutrition guidance for healing and milk supply

Additional Services

  • Weight loss & wellness services
  • Virtual primary care (TX, NM, UT, AZ, NV)
  • Sick visits
  • Chronic disease management
  • Medication refills
  • Preventive care

Meet Your Provider

I'm Obi Eke, MSN, APRN, FNP-C

A Family Nurse Practitioner passionate about postpartum care, women's health, and holistic wellness.

With years of clinical experience in primary care and women's health, I created Bright Bloom Wellness to fill the gap mothers often face after delivery — when they are discharged early, overwhelmed at home, and expected to recover without support.

I provide compassionate, culturally attuned postpartum care with a focus on:

  • Preventing complications
  • Supporting physical & emotional healing
  • Educating and empowering mothers
  • Reducing unnecessary ER visits
  • Helping women transition confidently into motherhood
